# Overview
For the final project in my Taxonomy and Ontology creation class, we built an ontology (if you're unfamiliar, essentially a graph database) of different devices that one could find in an IoT equipped kitchen. We focused on commonalities across devices, kinds of relationships that might connect them together, and how they may be connected to other areas of the home (through domains like power management or a HVAC system).

Our project was evaluated on the relationships that we identified between our devices, and how effectively we connected to other domains. We then gave a presentation to the class explaining our rationale for our system of organization, and explained the value our ontology would provide to an end-consumer or a business.
